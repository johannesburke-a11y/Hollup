## 🧠 How to Prompt Effectively

> *The core skill that separates good AI use from frustrating AI use.*

### The Anatomy of a Good Prompt

| Element | Purpose | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Role** | Tell the AI who it is | *"You are an HR communications specialist..."* |
| **Context** | Give relevant background | *"I'm writing an email to a team that just completed onboarding..."* |
| **Task** | State exactly what you want | *"Write a 3-paragraph summary of..."* |
| **Format** | Specify the output structure | *"Use bullet points. Keep it under 150 words."* |
| **Constraints** | Set guardrails | *"Do not include specific names or costs."* |

### Prompting Tips

- ✅ **Be specific** — vague prompts produce vague outputs
- ✅ **Iterate** — treat it like a conversation; refine based on what you get
- ✅ **Assign a role** — "Act as a..." often improves quality significantly
- ✅ **Ask for options** — "Give me 3 versions of..."
- ❌ **Don't accept the first output blindly** — always review
- ❌ **Don't input sensitive data** to improve a prompt

## ❌ Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

| Mistake | Why It Happens | How to Avoid It |
|---|---|---|
| Accepting output without reviewing | Output sounds confident even when wrong | Always fact-check key claims |
| Prompts that are too vague | Unclear what outcome is needed | Use the RCTFC framework above |
| Entering sensitive data | Habit from other tools | Read the guardrails section before every new task type |
| Over-relying on AI for decisions | AI feels authoritative | AI informs — humans decide |

## 📎 Appendix

### A. Glossary of AI Terms

| Term | Plain-Language Definition |
|---|---|
| **Prompt** | The instruction or question you give to an AI tool |
| **LLM (Large Language Model)** | The type of AI that powers most text-based AI tools |
| **Hallucination** | When an AI confidently produces incorrect or fabricated information |
| **Context window** | How much text the AI can "remember" within one conversation |
| **Temperature** | A setting that controls how creative vs. consistent AI responses are |
| **Token** | The unit of text an AI processes (roughly ¾ of a word) |
