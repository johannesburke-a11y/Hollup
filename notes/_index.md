# Notes

> **Free thoughts without a fixed place.**

## Purpose

For thoughts, observations, and ideas that don't clearly fit anywhere yet. More permanent than the inbox (things you want to keep), but less structured than a wiki page.

## Difference from inbox

- **Inbox**: unclear, needs to be processed and moved
- **Notes**: intentionally loose, may stay here

## File naming

```
notes/<YYYY-MM-DD>-<topic>.md
# or
notes/<topic>.md     ← for evergreen notes
```

## Rules

- Notes may stay here indefinitely — but review them occasionally.
- When a note becomes a proper knowledge entry → move to `knowledge/wiki/`.
- When a note is about a project → move to `projects/<slug>/notes.md`.
