🚀 Introducing: Google Workspace Studio
Business IT is committed to provide IONOS with modern AI Tools for workflow automation, therefore we are excited to announce the rollout of Google Workspace Studio to all employees.
Google Workspace Studio is a dedicated space where you can build "Flows"—automated sequences and AI Agents that use Gemini’s reasoning power to handle manual work without writing a single line of code. Whether it’s automatically labeling priority emails or summarizing complex documents as they arrive in a Drive folder, Studio is here to act as your personal digital assistant.


What can you do with it?
Workflow Integration: Connect your Workspace across Chat, Mail, Sheets, Docs, Drive and Tasks leveraging the power of Gemini. Further integrations to Jira, Confluence, Salesforce etc. are planned.
Content Processing: Automatically extract information (e.g. invoice or customer numbers) from PDF attachments in Drive and save them to a Google Sheet.
Intelligent Workflows: Create custom flows triggered on a schedule or event-based.

🛠️ Getting Started & Setting up your Account
To ensure you can access and use these features, you must enable the Smart Features in your Google account:
Enable Smart Features
Workspace Studio requires "Smart Features" to be active to understand the context of your emails and files.
In your Gmail, go to Settings (gear icon) > See all settings.
In the General tab, scroll down to Smart features and personalization.
Ensure the box is Checked.
Scroll to the bottom and click Save Changes.
Access the Studio Portal
Once your settings are updated, you can access the automation builder directly at:
👉 https://studio.workspace.google.com/
